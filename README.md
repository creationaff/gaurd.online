# Family Shield - Simple Internet Protection

**Protect your family from Pornography, Malware, and Hacker Attacks with just ONE click.**

No technical skills required.

## How to Use (Super Simple)

### Mac Users
1. Download and unzip this folder.
2. **Double-click** the file named `run_blocker.sh`.
   *(If it asks for permission or opens in a text editor, right-click > Open With > Terminal).*
3. Type your computer password and press Enter.
4. The **Family Shield** window will appear. Click **"TURN ON PROTECTION"**.

### Windows Users
1. Download and unzip this folder.
2. Find the file `one_click_block.py`.
3. **Right-click** on it and choose **"Run as Administrator"**.
4. The **Family Shield** window will appear. Click **"TURN ON PROTECTION"**.

## What Does It Do?
- **Blocks Porn:** Stops adult websites from loading.
- **Blocks Reddit:** Prevents access to Reddit.
- **Blocks Hackers & AI Threats:** Uses advanced DNS filtering (Cloudflare Family) to stop malware and AI botnets.

## Made a Mistake?
Don't worry! Open the app again and click **"Turn Off / Restore Normal Settings"** to go back to normal.

## For Complete Home Protection
The app will open your **Router Settings** page in your browser.
To protect *every* device in your house (phones, tablets, gaming consoles), log in to your router and set the DNS to:
- **Primary DNS:** `1.1.1.3`
- **Secondary DNS:** `1.0.0.3`
